WHAT OUR PEOPLE SAY
JULIA LEUCKERMANN
CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT MANAGER, FACTORY UTRECHT
“One of the things I love about working in the Roastery is that I can go to the supermarket, see our products on the shelves and I’ll feel connected to the world through coffee. I can pick up a pack and read the label, and will know where it’s produced, when it was made – I might even know the colleagues who roasted it.”
